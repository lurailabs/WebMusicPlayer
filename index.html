Web Music Player

Drag and drop your music files to add them to the playlist. Nothing will travel to the server, everything happens in your browser. Track info and cover image is fetched from the audio file ID3 tags, when present. Click on the animation to change it.

Made by Lurai.
You can find me on Twitter, or take a look at the code on GitHub.
